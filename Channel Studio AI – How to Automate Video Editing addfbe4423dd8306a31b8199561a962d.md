# Channel Studio AI – How to Automate Video Editing

*(Super simple for 10-year-olds. Fits right into your existing workflow. AI does most of the hard work!)*

**Goal**: Turn your script + visuals + voiceover into a finished 2–5 minute faceless video with almost no manual timeline dragging.

**Easiest Automation Options (Pick One – Start Simple)**

**Best for Kids: CapCut (Free + Very Easy)**

CapCut has strong **AI Auto features** that kids can use quickly.6

**How to Automate in CapCut**:

1. Open CapCut (desktop or mobile – desktop is better for teams).
2. Import your **voiceover** first (it becomes your timeline guide).
3. Upload visuals/pictures/clips to the media bin.
4. Use **AutoCut** or **Auto Captions**:
    - AI adds captions, effects, and basic cuts automatically.
5. Use **AI Image Generator** inside CapCut for missing visuals.
6. Add template (search “educational explainer” or “faceless”).
7. AI handles transitions, text animations, and pacing.
8. Export MP4 – done in minutes!

**Even Easier: InVideo AI or Canva Magic Studio**

- Paste your **script** → AI generates a full video draft with visuals, voice, music, and transitions.4
- Then tweak lightly in the editor.

**Updated Video Project with Automation (Phase 3 Becomes Super Fast)**

**Phase 1: Planning (Day 1)** – Same as before (AI script).

**Phase 2: Gather Stuff (Day 2)**

- AI visuals + voiceover (ElevenLabs or CapCut TTS).

**Phase 3: Build Video – Now Automated (Day 3 – Much Shorter!)**

**Tasks & AI Help**:

- Import voiceover + visuals into **CapCut / Canva / InVideo**.
- Run **Auto Edit** features:
    - Auto captions
    - Auto transitions & effects
    - AI suggests B-roll or animations
- Reviewer kid watches and makes small fixes (change one scene, adjust timing).
- Export MP4 to Videos folder.

**Phase 4–6**: Same (SEO, Upload, Review).

**Simple Automation Table for Kids**

| **Task** | **AI Tool** | **How Kids Do It** | **Time Saved** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Add Captions | CapCut Auto Captions | One click → AI writes & styles them | Huge |
| Match visuals to script | InVideo / Canva Magic | Paste script → AI picks/places clips | Big |
| Transitions & Effects | CapCut Templates | Choose template → AI applies everything | Medium |
| Full video from script | InVideo AI or CapCut | Paste script + voice → AI builds draft | Massive |
| Thumbnail | Canva Magic / Grok Imagine | Type description → Generate | Fast |

**Pro Tips for Your Team**

- **Start with Templates**: Create one “AI Governance Style” template in CapCut/Canva. Reuse it every video.
- **Human Check**: AI is fast but kids must watch the full video and fix anything weird (keeps it educational and high quality).
- **Free First**: Use free tiers of CapCut + Canva. Add paid (like InVideo) later if needed.
- **Team Roles Update**:
    - Voice/Editor Kid → now “AI Video Runner” (runs the auto tools).
    - Reviewer still very important!

**Full Workflow Reminder with Automation**

1. Read Request → AI Script
2. AI Visuals + Voiceover
3. **Auto Edit in CapCut/Canva** (new fast step)
4. AI SEO (title/description)
5. Upload & Launch
6. Review & Improve

This makes video editing the **easiest** part instead of the hardest.
