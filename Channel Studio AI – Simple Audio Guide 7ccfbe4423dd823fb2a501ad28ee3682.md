# Channel Studio AI – Simple Audio Guide

**Why Audio Matters**: Good clear voice + calm music = better videos. People stay watching longer, and YouTube likes it.

**Phase 2 Update: Gather Audio (Day 2)**

**1. Voiceover (The Main Talking – AI Makes It Easy)**

**Best Free/ Easy Tools for Kids (2026)**:

- **CapCut Built-in TTS** → Easiest! Inside your video editor.
- **ElevenLabs** (free tier) → Most natural sounding voices.
- **Play.ht** or **TTSMaker** → Good free options with commercial rights.

**Super Simple Steps for Voiceover**:

1. Take your AI script from Phase 1.
2. Go to CapCut → **Text** tab → **Text-to-Speech** (or import from ElevenLabs).
3. Choose a clear, friendly voice (try a few).
4. Adjust **Speed** (not too fast), **Pitch**, and **Tone**.
5. Generate → Save as MP3/WAV to **Voiceovers** folder.
6. Play it back and fix script if needed.

**Tip**: Generate captions right after (from previous guide) while voice is clean.

**2. Background Music (The Quiet Feel-Good Sound)**

**Best Free Sources** (Royalty-Free = Safe for YouTube):

- **YouTube Audio Library** (studio.youtube.com → Audio Library) – Search “calm”, “educational”, “inspirational”.
- **Pixabay** or **Chosic** – Free downloads.
- CapCut built-in music library (check copyright).

**How to Add in CapCut**:

1. Import your voiceover first (it’s the main audio track).
2. Click **Audio** or **Music** tab.
3. Search and add a track (or drag from your computer).
4. Place it on a lower track so it plays under the voice.
5. Lower the music volume (50–70% or less) so voice is clear.
6. Add **Fade In / Fade Out** at start and end.
7. Trim music to match video length.

**Pro Kid Tip**: Use “Beat Sync” in CapCut if available – AI matches music beats to your cuts!

**3. Audio Fixes & Polish (Make It Sound Professional)**

In CapCut:

- **Noise Reduction** → Auto cleans background hiss.
- **Volume Leveler** → Keeps voice consistent.
- **Voice Enhancer** → Makes AI voices clearer.
- Split audio tracks if needed (e.g., lower music during important explanations).
- Add soft sound effects (e.g., gentle ding for key points) from CapCut library.

**Order on Timeline** (Bottom to Top):

- Voiceover (loudest)
- Background Music (quiet)
- Sound Effects (short & soft)

**Updated Phase 3: Build Video (With Audio)**

**Tasks**:

- Import voiceover
- Add & balance background music
- Run Auto Captions (uses clean voiceover)
- Fix audio levels
- Export MP4

**Simple Audio Checklist (Print & Use)**

**Voiceover**

- Script ready
- Generated with AI voice
- Sounds clear & friendly

**Music**

- Royalty-free track chosen
- Volume low enough
- Fades added

**Final Check**

- Voice louder than music
- No weird noises
- Full video sounds good on phone

**Team Roles Update**

- **Voice/Editor Kid** → Now also handles AI voice + music balance.
- Reviewer always listens on headphones/speakers.

This keeps audio simple but high quality. Most of it is now AI-assisted inside CapCut!
