# Manual Process: Getting Your YouTube Video Live

*(Kid/team-friendly checklist – after your video is edited and ready as MP4. This follows the previous pre-launch steps.)*

**Goal**: Upload your faceless video (e.g., AI Governance – Authorization) and make it **Public** so anyone can watch it.

**Super Simple 8-Step Process to Go Live**

**Step 1: Sign In & Open YouTube Studio**

- Go to [studio.youtube.com](https://studio.youtube.com/) on a computer (best for full control).
- Log in with your Google account.
- Make sure your channel is verified (youtube.com/verify) for longer videos and custom thumbnails.

**Step 2: Start the Upload**

- Click the big **CREATE** button (top right) → **Upload videos**.
- Drag your MP4 file into the box or click to select it.
- You can upload multiple files, but start with one.

**Step 3: Fill in the Details (While It Uploads)**

Work on these tabs as the video processes:

- **Title**: Catchy + keyword (e.g., “AI Authorization Explained Simply for Beginners”).
- **Description**: Hook + timestamps + links + call-to-action.
- **Thumbnail**: Upload your custom Canva design (1280x720).
- **Playlist**: Add to your series (e.g., “AI Governance”).
- **Tags**: 8–15 relevant ones.
- **Category**: Education.
- **Audience**: Usually “Not made for kids”.

**Step 4: Add Extras for Better Videos**

- **Subtitles/Captions**: Turn on auto-captions and edit them.
- **End Screen**: Add Subscribe button + next video link.
- **Cards**: Add pop-up info during the video.
- **Language & Location** if needed.

**Step 5: Go to Visibility (The “Go Live” Decision)**

- Click the **Visibility** tab.
- Choose:
    - **Public** → Video is live for everyone right away (or after processing).
    - **Set as Instant Premiere** → Creates a countdown and live-chat feel.
    - **Schedule** → Pick a future date/time (video stays private until then).
    - Unlisted/Private (for testing first).

**Step 6: Check Everything**

- Wait for processing (SD is fast; 1080p takes longer).
- Preview the video on phone + computer.
- Review title, thumbnail, description for mistakes.
- Check copyright/ad suitability warnings (YouTube scans automatically).

**Step 7: Publish!**

- Click **Save** or **Publish**.
- For Public/Premiere: It goes live once processing finishes.
- You’ll get a link to share immediately.

**Step 8: Post-Launch Quick Actions**

- Share the link on social media or in a community.
- Pin a comment (e.g., “What AI topic next?”).
- Watch analytics after a few hours.
- Save everything in your D:\Files\Output\Completed folder.

**Kid/Team Roles**

- **Uploader**: Handles the file and Studio clicks.
- **Details Kid**: Writes/fills title, description, tags.
- **Thumbnail Checker**: Uploads and approves image.
- **Reviewer**: Previews and says “Go!” or “Fix this”.

**Tips for Success**

- Use a computer for the best experience (mobile app is simpler but limited).
- Video must finish SD processing before it can go Public.
- First videos might take time to process — be patient!
- Always follow YouTube rules (no spam, copyright-free music/images).

This process is repeatable and matches the earlier video creation workflow.
