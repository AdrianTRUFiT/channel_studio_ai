# Channel Studio AI – CapCut Auto Captions Guide

*(Super simple for 10-year-olds. This makes your educational videos much better for YouTube!)*

**Why Use Auto Captions?**

- Helps people watch without sound.
- YouTube likes videos with captions.
- Makes your AI Governance videos easier to understand.

**Best Way: Use CapCut Desktop (Recommended for Team)**

**Step-by-Step: Generate Auto Captions**

1. Open your project in **CapCut Desktop**.
2. Make sure your **voiceover** is on the timeline (clean audio = better results).
3. Go to the **Text** tab (left sidebar or top menu).
4. Click **Auto Captions** (or Captions → Auto Captions).
5. Choose these **settings**:

**Key Settings for Your Videos** (Best for educational faceless content):

- **Spoken Language**: English (or your main language). Use “Auto Detect” first if unsure.
- **Identify Different Speakers**: Turn **ON** if multiple voices (usually OFF for AI voiceovers).
- **Bilingual Captions**: Turn **OFF** for now (keeps it simple).
- **Auto Highlight Keywords**: Turn **ON** – AI makes important words (like “Authorization”) stand out.
- **AI Emojis**: Turn **ON** or **OFF** (fun for kids, but maybe OFF for serious AI topics).
- **Identify Filler Words**: Turn **ON** – removes “um”, “ah”, etc. for cleaner text.
- Click **Generate** (or Create). Wait 10–60 seconds.

Captions appear as a new track on the timeline.

**After Generating: Quick Kid-Friendly Edits**

- **Review & Fix**: Play the video. Click any caption box to edit text, timing, or split long lines.
- **Style All at Once**:
    - Select all captions (Ctrl+A or Cmd+A).
    - Change font (big, easy-to-read like Arial Bold).
    - Color: White text with dark shadow or background box.
    - Size: Big enough for phones.
    - Position: Lower third of screen (bottom).
- Use **Templates**: Go to Styles or Templates for cool animations (Typewriter, Pop, etc.). Apply to all captions with one click.
- **Batch Edit**: Great for fixing the same mistake everywhere (e.g., replace a wrong word).

**Pro Tip for Accuracy**:

- Use clean voiceover (no background music when generating).
- Generate captions **before** adding music.
- Always watch the full video and fix mistakes.

**Mobile App Version (Simpler but Less Control)**

1. Tap **Text** at bottom.
2. Tap **Auto Captions**.
3. Choose language → **Generate**.
4. Edit and style the same way.

**Updated Phase 3 (Build Video) with Captions**

- Import voice + visuals.
- Run **Auto Captions** with the settings above.
- Reviewer kid checks and fixes.
- Add music + final export.

This step takes kids just a few minutes now!
